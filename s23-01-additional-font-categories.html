19.1 Additional Font Categories

There are several font categories in addition to serif and sans serif fonts that may be utilized.

In earlier chapters, we discussed the difference between serif and sans serif fonts. However, as you might imagine, the story is far more complex than that. There are many more specific font categories. These categories are based on fonts that share certain characteristics. We consider five basic font classifications though there are more.
